Supra

The Supra is a sports car/grand tourer manufactured by Toyota Motor Corporation. The original Supra was derived from the Toyota Celica, but it was longer and wider. Starting in mid-1986, the A70 Supra became a separate model from the Celica.

The latest Toyota Supra, introduced in 2019, was co-developed with BMW and shares its platform with the BMW Z4. The Supra is renowned for its performance, handling, and iconic status among automotive enthusiasts.

Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, Italy. The company was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 and is known for producing high-performance vehicles that have become synonymous with speed and style.

Ferrari's cars are highly prized by collectors and are known for their distinctive design and performance capabilities. Some of the iconic models include the Ferrari 488, Ferrari LaFerrari, and Ferrari F40.

Bugatti

Bugatti is a French luxury automobile manufacturer that specializes in high-performance, luxury cars. Founded by the Italian-born French automobile designer Ettore Bugatti in 1909, Bugatti's cars are known for their engineering excellence and luxury.

Bugatti's models, including the Bugatti Veyron and Chiron, have set multiple speed records and are among the most expensive and exclusive cars in the world.
